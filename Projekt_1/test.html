Megadeth – Thrash metal legend

Megadeth is one of the most influential and enduring bands in the history of heavy metal. Formed in 1983 by guitarist and vocalist Dave Mustaine after his departure from Metallica, Megadeth became one of the pioneers of thrash metal, alongside bands like Slayer, Anthrax, and Metallica.

To get a sense of the band’s distinctive sound and emotional depth, listen to a fragment of one of their iconic tracks — “In My Darkest Hour.”
